Rafting Site Plan

Ugochukwu Onunkwo

Overview

Purpose

"The purpose of our website is to provide a comprehensive online platform for our rafting business, showcasing our services, and allowing customers to book and plan their rafting adventures with us. We aim to create an engaging and informative website that highlights the excitement and thrill of water rafting, while also providing a seamless user experience for our customers to plan and book their trips. Our website will serve as a hub for all information related to our rafting services, including trip options, pricing, safety guidelines, and customer testimonials. By having an online presence, we hope to expand our reach, increase bookings, and build a community of rafting enthusiasts."

Audience

"Our target audience is adventure-seeking individuals and groups, aged 18-50, who are interested in water rafting and outdoor activities. They are likely to be physically active, enjoy nature, and seeking thrilling experiences. They may be beginners or experienced rafters, and are looking for a reliable and safe water rafting service."
